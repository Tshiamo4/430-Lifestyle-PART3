About Us

430 Lifestyle is a streetwear brand dedicated to creating bold, stylish clothing that represents modern youth culture.

Founded with a passion for fashion and creativity, we design clothes that inspire confidence and individuality.

Brief History: Tshiamo Sereme, Nhlanhla Papole, and Lindo Msimango founded the youth-oriented brand 430 Lifestyle in 2023. The brand began as a streetwear and apparel concept that promoted creative culture and urban fashion in Gauteng. Pop-up events, partnerships, and social media interaction have all contributed to its growth.

Vision Statement: To establish ourselves as a preeminent urban lifestyle brand that promotes creativity, diversity, and constructive change.

Target Audience: Trendsetters, creatives, urban fashion enthusiasts, and young adults (15–30 years old).

Mission Statement: Our mission is to empower youth by fostering community, fashion, and creativity.
